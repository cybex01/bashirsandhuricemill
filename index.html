About Bashir Sandhu Rice Mill

Established over three decades ago, Bashir Sandhu Rice Mill has been at the forefront of rice processing excellence in Pakistan. Our commitment to quality and tradition has made us a trusted name among rice traders and exporters worldwide.

We specialize in processing premium paddy into the finest Super Basmati and PK-386 rice varieties. With state-of-the-art machinery and traditional expertise, we ensure every grain meets international quality standards.

Our Premium Rice Varieties

We specialize in processing two of Pakistan's finest rice varieties

Super Basmati Rice

World-renowned for its exceptional aroma, long grains, and fluffy texture. Perfect for special occasions and premium export markets.

  • Extra-long grain
  • Rich aroma

PK-386 Rice

A versatile variety known for its consistent quality and excellent cooking characteristics. Ideal for daily consumption and bulk orders.

Our Rice Processing Steps

From paddy to premium rice - our meticulous 5-step process

1

Cleaning

Removal of impurities, stones, and foreign materials using advanced cleaning machinery.

2

Husking

Gentle removal of outer husk while preserving the grain integrity and nutritional value.

3

Polishing

Multiple stage polishing to achieve the perfect shine and remove bran layers.

4

Grading

Precise sorting by size and quality to ensure consistency in every batch.

5

Packaging

Hygienic packaging in various sizes to maintain freshness and quality during transport.
